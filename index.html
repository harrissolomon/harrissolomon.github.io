Harris Solomon

I'm an anthropologist of medicine, science, and urban India.

My book, Metabolic Living, details the rise of metabolic illness in urban India. I am currently researching and writing about traumatic injuries from traffic accidents in Mumbai. Across these projects and others, I'm interested in connections between the body and its environments in contemporary India.

I teach in the Department of Cultural Anthropology at Duke University.
